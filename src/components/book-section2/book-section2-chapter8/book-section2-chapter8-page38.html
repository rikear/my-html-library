Then, we add some text on top that you can scroll to see mix-blend-mode in effect. In this example. we have used overlay, for no reason other than I thought it worked best. If you open the example along with your developer tools, you can cycle through the other possibilities. It's perhaps redundant to say so, but the options themselves will have different effects, depending on the foreground and background they are being applied to.

The specification for mix-blend-mode is the Compositing and Blending Level 1 spec-ification: https: //www.w3.org/TR/compositing-1/#mix-blend-mode.
